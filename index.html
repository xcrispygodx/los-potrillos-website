Our Story

Tradition Since 2011

Welcome to Taqueria Los Potrillos, where family traditions come to life on every plate. Since 2011, we've been serving the Pleasanton community authentic Mexican cuisine made from recipes passed down through generations.

Every taco, every salsa, every dish is prepared with fresh, quality ingredients and the love that only a family kitchen can provide.
